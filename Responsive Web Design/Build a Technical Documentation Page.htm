Water Enjoyment Guide
Enjoy the Ocean on the water Enjoy the Ocean below the water Enjoy a lake Enjoy a river Essential equipment
Get out and enjoy your water way! Here's how to for each type
Enjoy the Ocean on the water

There are many great ways to enjoy the ocean on the water.

Sail and power boating, kayaking, or just swimming. Don't forget cruise ships!

Enjoy the Ocean below the water

The ocean has abundant life just below the serface.

Grab your scuba gear and get under there!

Enjoy a lake

Lakes have the same opportunites as the ocean, like sailing and power boating, kayaking, and swimming.

The relative calmness of some lakes also make canoeing possible too!

Enjoy a river

Large rivers are deep enough for boats, but many are only deep enough for canoes and kayaks.

Perhaps the best way to enjoy a river is from an innertube. Don't forget a tube for your cooler!

Essential equipment

There is some essential equipment you'll need for any trip on the water.

Safety first! Let's make sure you have essential life-saving devices.

  • Life Jacket
  • Whistle
  • First Aid Kit
  • Flares
  • Flashlight
  • Fire Extinguisher, if on a boat with a motor
